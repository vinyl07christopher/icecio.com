Conference Scope


International Conference on Evolutionary Computation for Intelligent Optimization “International Conference on Evolutionary Computation for Intelligent Optimization” highlights the most recent scientific inventions and uses of optimization algorithms in various fields of Computer Science, Engineering and Information Technology. A wide range of evolutionary computational models has been developed, including evolutionary algorithms, genetic algorithms, fuzzy optimization, evolutionary programming, memetic Algorithms and swarm intelligence. Intelligent computing is significantly widened the scope for computing, extending it from traditional computing into increasingly efficient intelligent optimized frameworks. Evolutionary computation intelligent optimization is amultidisciplinary study that employs evolutionary computation techniques to extract and accumulate relevant patterns in order to develop intelligent computing and its relative applications. This series includes proceedings in the field of intelligent computing such as : Artificial Intelligence, Deep Neural Networks, Game Theory, Quantum Computing, cloud computing, and Optimization such as Ant Colony Optimization, Chaos Optimization, Continuous Optimization, Genetic Algorithms Particle Swarm Optimization, Pattern Search. The conference also intends to promote hybrid techniques within evolutionary computation, as well as fuzzy and neural computation. The aim of this conference is to provide an opportunity for researchers to present their innovative and unpublished research works on evolutionary computation with next- generation intelligent applications. Research findings, extensive case studies and industrial experience based on the scope are invited for submission.
